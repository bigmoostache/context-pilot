A COWORKER, NOT A CHATBOT

Delegate a project. Walk away. Come back to progress.

Working with Daharness feels less like prompting a tool and more like handing work to a teammate who keeps going after you log off.

  1. 01

    Post an agent to a project

    Point an agent at a project folder and hand it the work — research to gather, a document to draft, a backlog to clear. It sets up its own workspace and gets going, no babysitting required.

  2. 02

    Step away

    Head into a meeting, or start another project of your own. Your agents keep working and checking their own results, pausing only when they genuinely need a decision from you.

  3. 03

    Check in from anywhere

    Glance at the whole team from the browser cockpit, or drop into any single agent from your terminal. Review, nudge, redirect — then leave them to it again.

SOFTWARE, MEET HARDWARE

Run Daharness on your machines — or on a box of its own.

Daharness is built on Context Pilot, the open-source harness that keeps its agents sharp. Run it on your own machines, or let the Daharness appliance — a small dedicated box — host the whole team for you, alongside ready-to-use work environments. Nothing to rack, nothing to configure.

The appliance fits wherever your team already is, in two modes.

Plug in→ Connect→ Delegate
MODE 1 On your network

Drop it on your home or company network. It joins the LAN and puts Daharness and its work environments within reach of everyone already connected.

  • Joins your existing network
  • Agents & work environments, shared
  • Reach it from any device on the LAN
MODE 2 Its own network
5G · private Wi-Fi

No network to join? Built-in 5G brings it online anywhere there's signal, and it broadcasts its own Wi-Fi — connectivity and Daharness from the same box.

  • 5G uplink — online anywhere
  • Broadcasts its own Wi-Fi
  • Daharness on a private network
